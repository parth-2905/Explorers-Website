Day 01: Arrive Leh

In the evening, visit Leh Market, Leh Palace, and Shanti Stupa.

Post breakfast, we proceed for the Indus Valley Monastery Tour, including Shey, the ancient capital of Ladakh, and Thiksey, an impressive complex rising tier upon tier on a hill above the village.

Overnight stay at hotel in Leh.

Day 03

Day 03: Leh ‚Äì Sham Valley Tour

Post breakfast, we drive on the Srinagar Highway and start our day by visiting the Hall of Fame ‚Äì a museum constructed by the Indian Army.
