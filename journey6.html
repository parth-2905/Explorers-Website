Day 03

Uleytokpo to Leh (125 km / 5 hrs)

Post breakfast, we drive further to visit Lamayuru Monastery and then head back to Leh.
Overnight stay at hotel in Leh.

Day 04

Visit the Diskit Monastery and the sand dunes, where you can enjoy a camel safari (optional).
Check in at the resort and enjoy your evening at the campsite.
Overnight stay in Nubra.

Tour concludes.
