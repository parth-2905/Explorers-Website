About Us

Discover the story behind Explorers

INTRODUCTION

Explorers, founded in 2008, is a Destination Management Company based in Leh – Ladakh with a marketing and sales office in the NCR of Delhi.

Explorers's quality services for both F.I.T. and GROUPS include high value accommodations, tours, transfers and guiding services.

We specialize in creating tailor made itineraries and have years of experience in organizing adventure, cultural, special interest tours and corporate tours.

Explorers is a team of driven professionals with a passion for experience led travel. Our goal simply is to create experience-led products, allowing our customers to make the most of their journeys.

The corporate office is located in Leh with an operations team that successfully handled 2900 pax in 2015. Our sales and marketing office in the NCR is headed by a very strong team of sales and customer care, who ensure quality customer care to our network of B2B sales and also our own loyalty base.

WHY US?

  • We are a local travel company registered in Leh with a sales office in the NCR of Delhi.
  • We have a very strong local connect with hospitality partners all across the region.
  • We manage and run one hotel in Leh and a camp in Nubra.
  • We have successfully handled Group Tours, FIT, Special Interest Tours, Corporate and adventure tours.
  • We create bespoke tours that take you beyond the roads to villages and village Homestays.
  • We organise adventure tours ranging from trekking, motorbike and rafting expeditions to soft adventures, such as village walks and horse rides.

KEY COMPANIES

Explorers has successfully handled corporate travel for some of the biggest names in the industry, partnering with renowned travel companies across India.
